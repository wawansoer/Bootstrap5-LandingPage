Automate in 3 Simple Steps

Getting started with CloudFlow is quick and easy. Follow these steps to begin automating your repetitive tasks and connecting your applications.

Connect Your Apps

Securely link your favorite cloud applications to CloudFlow using our guided authorization process. Your data's security is our priority.

Build Your Flow

Choose a trigger app and event that starts your workflow. Then, use our visual builder to add actions, logic, and filters.

Activate & Relax

Test your workflow to make sure it runs as expected. Once you're happy, activate it and let CloudFlow handle the rest automatically.
